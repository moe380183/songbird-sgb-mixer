Crypto Mixer and Wallet Obfuscation

The purpose of this project is to provide a mixer and wallet obfuscation service in a cost effective and fast way.

Why Obfuscate?

As crypto currencies have gained popularity there appears to be a need to for crypto holders to remain as anonymous as possible in order to prevent said holders becoming targets of unsavoury individuals.

Wallet obfuscation and mixers can help in this way, as you can disconnect funds source from your private unknown wallet.

Current State Of Play

Many mixers currently do exists, but it appears that you would have to tie your cryptocurrency in such services over a period of time, while having to pay high network fees.

Project Aims

By leveraging the Songbird Network we feel we could build a service that is cheaper and faster to help obfuscate crypto assets.

Requirements

We will initially support the Metamask wallet in order to interact with a smart contract using web3.js.

Source Wallet

This is the wallet that will depot funds in to the smart contract. This wallet is the source of the crypto asset.

Destination Wallet

This is a wallet that your crypto will be sent to. It can be the funds source, but its recommended that it's a different wallet. We can use this wallet to see if we could trace back the funds from the source wallet.
